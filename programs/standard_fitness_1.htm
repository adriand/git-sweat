Standard Fitness 1

Push-up: 25,15,15

Planche both arms: 30,20,20 Keeping body straight, hold yourself up with both arms.

Crunch: 30,20,15

Squat: 40,30,30

Leg raises: 25,20,15 Lay flat on the ground. With hands supporting your lower back, raise your legs.

Bicep curls: 30,30,20 Using weights or resistance bands, do bicep curls.
